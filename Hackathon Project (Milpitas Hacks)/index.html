Frequently Asked Questions

These are questions that people tend to have about our product!

1. How accurate is your AI in assessing my emotional state based on text alone?

Our AI uses advanced natural language processing and a sentimental analysis technique to assese the emotional state. While of course its not perfect it will learn to continously improve its accurary.

2. What measures do you have in place to ensure the investment advice is reliable and not just based on mood?

Our Ai combines emotional analysis with robust financial algorithims and market data. This dual approach allows decisions to be grounded both emotionally and finacially.

3. How does the AI differentiate between temporary emotions and long-term emotional states?

The AI analyzes patterns in text over time, consistent emotional trends verus short-term fluctuation. Looking at historical data and data sets aswell as recent inputs to adjust its recommendation.

4. Can the AI's recommendations be trusted more than those of a human financial advisor?

Our AI offers a blend of emotional intelligence and financial expertise, providing something that is more of a complement to traditional advisors. While we don't recommend to chose one, its better to research before making a decision.

5. What safeguards are in place to protect my personal data and ensure privacy?

We prioritize your privacy and data security. Using a encryption and follows strict data protection to ensure your information is secure.
